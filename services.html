    FAQ

  • What is MIDI Rework?

    Not satisfied with the pattern or variety of your MIDI tracks? I will enhance or rework your MIDI tracks to elevate the intensity and impact of your music

  • What is Detailed Vocal Tuning?

    With detailed vocal tuning, I will intricately address fastidious pitch discrepancies to ensure your vocals sound professional and natural.I strive to maintain the authenticity and emotional expression of the original performance while achieving precise pitch correction and seamless integration.

  • What’s the difference between Key & Detailed vocal tuning?

    • Your vocals will undergo standard tuning with the same software used in detailed vocal tuning. However, I will not perform extensive modifications, such as altering the pitch of individual syllables, which is customary in detailed vocal tuning.
